BYOD (Bring Your Own Domain)

BYOD allows you to make your own domain for Benrogo.net! All it requires you to do is make some DNS entries and submit your domain!

First, make a DNS A record to this IP address:
5.161.233.190

If you need to use IPv6 too, make an AAAA record to
2a01:4ff:f0:dd7c::1

If you're using Cloudflare, TURN PROXY OFF

AFTER making these records, put your domain below and click submit! Please note that depending on your DNS provider it can take up to 24 hours for your site to come online, but most will work almost immediately!
